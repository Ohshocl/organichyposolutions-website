DIRECTIONS FOR USE

FOR ACNE & BLEMISHES: Apply a small amount directly to affected areas after cleansing. Use twice daily (morning and evening) for best results. Allow to absorb fully before applying other products.

FOR WOUNDS & CUTS: Clean area gently. Apply serum directly to wound. Cover with bandage if needed. Reapply 2-3 times daily until healed. Thicker formula stays in place longer than sprays.

FOR ATHLETE'S FOOT: Clean and dry feet thoroughly. Apply to affected areas between toes and on feet. Use morning and night. Continue for 2 weeks after symptoms improve.

FOR ECZEMA & IRRITATION: Apply thin layer to irritated skin. Gentle enough for sensitive areas. Safe for children and adults. Can be used as needed throughout the day.

PRECAUTIONS & STORAGE

PRECAUTIONS: For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes - if contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water. If irritation develops or persists, discontinue use and consult healthcare provider. Keep out of reach of children.

STORAGE: Store at room temperature (60-85°F) away from direct sunlight. Keep bottle tightly closed when not in use. Do not freeze. Product remains effective for 12 months from manufacture date when stored properly.
