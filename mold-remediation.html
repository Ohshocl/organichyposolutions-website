Why Mold Is a Serious Health Risk

Mold isn’t just an aesthetic problem — it’s a documented health hazard that escalates the longer it goes untreated

🫙
Respiratory Complications

Mold spores can irritate the airways, causing coughing, wheezing, shortness of breath, and aggravation of asthma — particularly in children and the elderly.

🫧
Allergic Reactions

Common mold exposure symptoms include sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes, and skin irritation. For sensitized individuals, reactions can be severe and persistent.

☣️
Toxic Mold Exposure

Certain molds — including black mold (Stachybotrys) — produce mycotoxins that can lead to more severe health effects, including neurological symptoms and immune suppression.

🫠
Sinus & Lung Infections

Prolonged exposure to mold spores can lead to chronic sinus infections and, in severe cases, lung infections like aspergillosis — particularly dangerous for immunocompromised individuals.

👁️
Eye & Skin Irritation

Direct contact with mold-contaminated surfaces or airborne spores can cause redness, itching, and irritation to both eyes and skin — often mistaken for allergies or other conditions.

🛡️
Risks to the Immunocompromised

People with compromised immune systems — including the elderly, infants, pregnant women, and those with chronic illness — face significantly higher risk of serious infection from mold exposure.

Moderate Jobs Most Common
  • Mold into drywall, wood framing, or insulation — selective demolition required
  • Semi-accessible: finished walls, behind paneling, under flooring
  • Multiple rooms — more containment setup, longer on-site time
  • Established colony, not just surface film
  • Moisture source active or recently repaired
$12–$15 /sq ft
Complex Jobs
  • Attics, crawl spaces, or HVAC plenums — crew access requires cutting or crawling
  • Black mold (Stachybotrys) — specialized handling required
  • Structural penetration — framing, subfloor, or insulation
  • Full containment with airlocks + clearance testing
  • Post-flood or water-damage jobs — high spore load, high labor intensity
