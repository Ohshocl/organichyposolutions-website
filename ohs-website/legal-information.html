Product Safety Information

USDA Certified Organic HOCl Safety Profile:
  • Safe for food contact surfaces (FDA Food Contact Notification 1811)
  • No rinse required on food contact surfaces
  • Safe around children and pets when used as directed
  • Biodegrades to harmless salt water
  • No toxic fumes or residues

Emergency Precautions

  • Eye Contact: Flush thoroughly with water for 15 minutes. Seek medical attention if irritation persists
  • Skin Contact: Rinse with water. Product is non-irritating to intact skin
  • Ingestion: Safe if accidentally ingested in small amounts. Drink water if concerned
  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air. Product vapor is non-toxic
