All-In-One Hypochlorous Wipes — Directions & Safety Information
PRECAUTIONARY STATEMENTS

Do not use with other household chemicals. No Personal Protective Equipment such as gloves, goggles, masks or respirators are required for use. Store in original container. Keep lid closed when not in use.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

GENERAL CLEANING & DEODORIZING: Wipe surface thoroughly. Allow surface to remain visibly wet for 10 minutes. Allow to air dry or wipe dry with a clean cloth. No rinsing necessary. For visibly soiled surfaces, pre-cleaning is recommended.

HARD SURFACES: Use on countertops, tables, appliances, toys, pet areas, and high-touch surfaces. Wipe surface completely. Allow to remain wet for 10 minutes. For heavily soiled areas, pre-clean and use multiple wipes as needed.

ELECTRONICS: Completely power off equipment prior to treatment. Pre-clean soils with dry cloth, then carefully apply. Avoid over-soaking and pooled areas. Treated surfaces must remain visibly wet for 10 minutes. Do not restore power until thoroughly dry.

FABRIC & UPHOLSTERY: Test on inconspicuous area first. Gently wipe surface. Safe for carpets, furniture, car interiors, and pet bedding.

STORAGE AND DISPOSAL

STORAGE: Store at room temperature (60–85°F) in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep lid tightly sealed to prevent wipes from drying out. Use within 120 days of production date or test with chlorine test strip to verify minimum 163 ppm FAC before use.

DISPOSAL: Wipes are biodegradable but should not be flushed. Dispose in household trash. Hypochlorous acid naturally breaks down into harmless salt water. Empty containers can be recycled where facilities exist.
