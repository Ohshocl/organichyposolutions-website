PRECAUTIONARY STATEMENTS

Do not use with other household chemicals. No Personal Protective Equipment such as gloves, goggles, masks or respirators are required for use. Store in original container. Keep lid closed when not in use.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

GENERAL CLEANING & DEODORIZING: Wipe surface thoroughly with cloth. Allow surface to remain visibly wet for 10 minutes for maximum effectiveness. Allow to air dry or wipe dry with a clean cloth. No rinsing necessary, even on food contact surfaces. For visibly soiled surfaces, pre-cleaning is recommended.

HARD NON-POROUS SURFACES: Use on countertops, tables, appliances, toys, pet areas, and high-touch surfaces. Wipe surface completely. For heavily soiled areas, use multiple wipes as needed.

ELECTRONIC EQUIPMENT: Safe for use on keyboards, phones, tablets, and touchscreens. Ensure excess moisture doesn't enter device openings. Allow to air dry completely before use.

FABRIC & UPHOLSTERY: Test on inconspicuous area first. Gently wipe fabric surfaces. Safe for use on carpets, furniture, car interiors, and pet bedding. Effective at removing odors and light stains.

STORAGE AND DISPOSAL

STORAGE: Store at room temperature (60-85°F) in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep lid tightly sealed to prevent wipes from drying out. Do not store above 100°F or below 32°F.

DISPOSAL: Wipes are biodegradable but should not be flushed. Dispose of used wipes in household trash. The hypochlorous acid naturally breaks down into harmless salt water after use. Empty containers can be recycled where facilities exist.

SHELF LIFE: Best if used within 12 months of manufacture date printed on package. Product remains effective when stored properly.
