HOW TO USE

GENERAL CLEANING & DEODORIZING: Wipe surface thoroughly. Allow surface to air dry or wipe dry with a clean cloth. No rinsing necessary. For visibly soiled surfaces, pre-cleaning is recommended.

HARD SURFACES: Use on countertops, tables, appliances, toys, pet areas, and high-touch surfaces. Wipe surface completely. For heavily soiled areas, use multiple wipes as needed.

ELECTRONICS: Safe for use on keyboards, phones, tablets, and touchscreens. Ensure excess moisture doesn't enter device openings. Allow to air dry completely before use.

FABRIC & UPHOLSTERY: Test on inconspicuous area first. Gently wipe fabric surfaces. Safe for carpets, furniture, car interiors, and pet bedding. Effective at removing odors and light stains.

STORAGE AND DISPOSAL

STORAGE: Store at room temperature (60–85°F) in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep lid tightly sealed to prevent wipes from drying out.

DISPOSAL: Wipes are biodegradable but should not be flushed. Dispose in household trash. Hypochlorous acid naturally breaks down into harmless salt water. Empty containers can be recycled where facilities exist.

SHELF LIFE: Best if used within 12 months of manufacture date printed on package.
