Our Story: Revolutionizing Sanitization

Founded by Brandon O'Neal and Bob Strang, Organic HypoSolutions (OHSHOCL) emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: bringing one of the safest, most effective disinfectant on Earth to every business and home in Utah.

We discovered that your own immune system produces one of the world's most powerful disinfectant - hypochlorous acid (HOCl). This revelation led us to pioneer commercial HOCl sanitization services that are:

  • 100x more effective than bleach
  • Completely safe for babies, pets, and food
  • USDA Certified Organic
  • EPA registered for hospital use

As Utah's only dual-certified HOCl provider, we're not just another cleaning company - we're health advocates bringing medical-grade sanitization to everyday spaces.

Our Mission

Transform global health standards by making medical-grade sanitization accessible, affordable, and environmentally responsible for every business and family.

Our Vision

A world where effective disinfection never requires toxic chemicals, where families breathe easier, and where businesses thrive in genuinely healthy environments.
