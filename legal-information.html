Health & Safety Information

Product Safety Profile

Hypochlorous Acid (HOCl) Safety Characteristics:
  • pH Neutral: Products maintain pH 6.5-7.5, gentler than most tap water
  • Food Safe: FDA approved for direct food contact without rinsing (FCN 1811)
  • Non-Toxic: Same molecule produced by white blood cells for natural immune defense
  • Biodegradable: Breaks down to harmless salt water after use
  • No PPE Required: Safe to use without gloves, masks, or respirators during normal application

Usage Guidelines and Precautions

  • Normal Use: Products are safe for normal household and commercial use as directed
  • Sensitive Individuals: Those with severe chemical sensitivities should test in small area first
  • Professional Application: Commercial fogging services performed by trained technicians
  • Storage: Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight for maximum shelf life
  • Compatibility: Safe around children, pets, and pregnant women when used as directed

Emergency Information (Minimal Risk)

First Aid Procedures (Precautionary):
  • Eye Contact: Rinse with clean water. Product is non-irritating but flush as precaution
  • Skin Contact: Rinse with water if desired. Product is gentle and non-irritating to intact skin
  • Ingestion: Safe if accidentally ingested in small amounts. Drink water if concerned
  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air. Vapor is non-toxic but may be mildly irritating in high concentrations

Note: These are precautionary measures. HOCl is recognized as extremely safe with minimal risk profile.
