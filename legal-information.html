Health & Safety Disclaimers

Important Medical Disclaimer

Not intended for medical diagnosis or treatment. Our sanitization services are for environmental disinfection only and are not medical treatments. Consult healthcare professionals for medical conditions or concerns.

Service Performance Disclaimers

  • Results may vary based on conditions: Environmental factors, contamination levels, and surface types affect treatment effectiveness
  • Professional application recommended: While our products are safe, professional application ensures optimal results and safety
  • Consult professionals for specific needs: Specialized applications may require additional expertise or equipment
  • Pre-existing conditions: Some stains, odors, or contamination may require multiple treatments or specialized approaches

Allergen Treatment Limitations

Important Allergen Information:
  • HOCl effectively eliminates many allergens but may not remove all allergenic particles
  • Individuals with severe allergies should consult medical professionals before treatment
  • Air filtration and source removal may be necessary for comprehensive allergen control
  • Results may vary based on allergen type and contamination level

Product Safety Information

USDA Certified Organic HOCl Safety Profile:
  • Safe for food contact surfaces (FDA Food Contact Notification 1811)
  • No rinse required on food contact surfaces
  • Safe around children and pets when used as directed
  • Biodegrades to harmless salt water
  • No toxic fumes or residues

Emergency Precautions

  • Eye Contact: Flush thoroughly with water for 15 minutes. Seek medical attention if irritation persists
  • Skin Contact: Rinse with water. Product is non-irritating to intact skin
  • Ingestion: Safe if accidentally ingested in small amounts. Drink water if concerned
  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air. Product vapor is non-toxic
