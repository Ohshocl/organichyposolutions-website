FSIS Compliance & Food Processing Legal Framework

FSIS Directive 7120 Compliance

Regulatory Authorization: Our hypochlorous acid products are listed in FSIS Directive 7120 as approved antimicrobial substances for use in meat and poultry processing establishments under USDA jurisdiction.

Applicability: Products approved for use in FSIS-regulated establishments processing meat, poultry, and egg products under 9 CFR Parts 416 and 424.

Approved Applications and Concentration Limits

FSIS-Approved HOCl Applications (Electrolytically Generated Hypochlorous Acid):
Application Maximum Concentration Reference
Red Meat Carcasses (spray) ≤50 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Poultry Carcasses (spray) ≤50 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Meat Processing Water ≤5 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Poultry Processing Water ≤50 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Poultry Chiller Water ≤50 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Poultry Chiller Red Water ≤5 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120
Giblets and Salvage Parts ≤50 ppm FAC FSIS Directive 7120

Note: FAC = Free Available Chlorine. Our product at 163-200 ppm concentration must be diluted appropriately for specific FSIS-approved applications.
