About Organic Hypo Solutions

Utah's trusted provider of USDA Certified Organic hypochlorous acid sanitization services.

Founded by Brandon O'Neal and Bob Strang, Organic Hypo Solutions brings the power of nature's own disinfectant to businesses and homes across Utah. Our mission is to provide the safest, most effective sanitization available while protecting the environment.

Why Hypochlorous Acid Works

Hypochlorous acid (HOCl) is naturally produced by your white blood cells to fight infection. When created through our specialized process, it becomes a powerful EPA-registered disinfectant that maintains exceptional safety standards.

Key Benefits:
  • ⚡ EPA-registered effectiveness against bacteria, viruses, and fungi
  • 👶 Safe for food contact without rinsing required
  • 🌱 Biodegrades to harmless salt water - environmentally responsible
  • 🦠 99.9% pathogen elimination on hard, non-porous surfaces
  • 🏥 Hospital disinfectant classification with residential safety
  • 🌍 WHO Essential Medicine recognition
