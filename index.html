Why HOCl Works

Hypochlorous acid (HOCl) is naturally produced by your white blood cells to fight infection. When created commercially, it becomes a powerful disinfectant that's:

  • 100x more effective than bleach against pathogens
  • Safe for food contact without rinsing
  • Gentle enough for babies and pets
  • Biodegrades to harmless salt water
