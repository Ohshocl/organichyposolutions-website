Our Mission

Founded by Brandon O'Neal and Bob Strang, Organic HypoSolutions delivers EPA-registered, USDA certified organic sanitization solutions to food processing facilities, healthcare institutions, agricultural operations, and residential properties across Utah. Our mission is to provide the safest, most effective sanitization available while protecting people, animals, and the environment.

From food processing protocols to healthcare-grade disinfection and agricultural biosecurity, we utilize advanced hypochlorous acid technology and specialized application methods tailored to each industry's unique contamination challenges and regulatory requirements.
