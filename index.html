Our Mission

Founded by Brandon O'Neal and Bob Strang, Organic Hyposolutions brings EPA-registered, USDA Certified Organic disinfection technology to businesses and homes across Utah. Our mission is to provide the safest, most effective sanitization available while protecting the environment.

Why HOCl Works

Hypochlorous acid (HOCl) is naturally produced by white blood cells to fight infection. When created commercially through electrolysis, it becomes a powerful EPA-registered disinfectant that maintains exceptional safety standards.
