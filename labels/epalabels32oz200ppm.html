PRECAUTIONARY STATEMENTS

Do not use with other household chemicals. No Personal Protective Equipment such as gloves, goggles, masks or respirators are required for use.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

It is a violation of Federal law to use this product in a manner inconsistent with its labeling.

HARD NON-POROUS SURFACE DISINFECTION: Spray, Sponge, Wipe, Dip, Mop or Immerse on surface. Allow surface to remain visibly wet for 10 minutes. Allow to air dry or wipe dry with a clean microfiber cloth, dish towel, or paper towel. No rinsing necessary, even on food contact surfaces. For visibly soiled surfaces, pre-cleaning is required.

ELECTRONIC EQUIPMENT: Power off equipment prior to treatment. Pre-clean with a cloth slightly wetted with this product. Apply using cloth or spray. Surfaces must remain wet for 10 minutes. Do not restore power until thoroughly dry.

GENERAL CLEANING AND DEODORIZING: Spray until visibly wet. Let stand for ten minutes. Then wipe dry.
