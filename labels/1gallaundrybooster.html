DIRECTIONS FOR USE

STANDARD LOAD: Pour 1-2 oz of Hypochlorous Laundry Booster into the fabric softener dispensing cup of your washing machine.

HEAVILY SOILED/ODOROUS LOADS: Pour 1-2 oz into fabric softener cup, PLUS an additional 2 oz into the bleach dispensing cup. Wash in warm water for best results.

IMPORTANT: No presoak or pretreat necessary. Use your regular laundry detergent as normal. Run load on any cycle, but for best results use warm and/or cold water. Dry as normal.

Each 1-gallon bottle contains approximately 64 loads (2oz per load).

⚠ FABRIC COMPATIBILITY WARNING

TEST FABRICS TO ASSURE COMPATIBILITY BEFORE USE. Do not use bleach or fabric softener when using Hypochlorous Laundry Booster. Do not use any other laundry products other than normal laundry detergent when using Laundry Booster.
